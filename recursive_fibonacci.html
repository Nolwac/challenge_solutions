Why is it a bad idea to use recursion method to find the fibonacci of a number

$$T(n) = T(n-1) + T(n-2) + c$$ $$ = 2T(n-2) + c$$ since from the approximation $T(n-1) \approx T(n-2)$ $$= 2 \times (2T(n-4) + c) + c$$ $$= 4T(n-4) + 3c$$ $$= 8T(n-6) + 7c$$ $$= 2^k \times T(n - 2k) + (2^k - 1) \times c$$ The last call in the call stack is when $n - 2k = 0$ hence we find the value of k at that point when $n - 2k = 0$ $$k = n/2$$ $$T(n) = 2^{n/2} \times T(0) + (2^{n/2} - 1) \times c$$ $$= 2^{n/2} \times (1 + c) - c$$ i.e. $$T(n) \approx 2^{n/2}$$
